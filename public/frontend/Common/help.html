FAQs

How do I register a field?

Open Register Field from the header or Features page, fill out the form, place the pin on the map, upload required documents, and submit for SRA review.

How can I join a field as a worker?

Go to the Lobby page, view the map, then click a field pin near you. If you want to join, click Join Field and wait for the field handlers to accept your request.

How to reset my password?

Use the Forgot Password option on the login page to receive a reset email. Check your spam folder if you don’t see it.

What is the Driver Badge?

The Driver Badge verifies professional drivers so they can access transport assignments, route viewing, and transport logs in CaneMap.

How do I apply for a Driver Badge?

Open Driver Badge from the Features page, fill in your details, and upload required documents (e.g., valid ID and vehicle registration if applicable). Submit for review.

How long is the Driver Badge review?

Reviews typically take 3–5 working days. You will receive a notification once a decision is made.

What documents are needed for field application?

Common requirements include Barangay Certification, Land Title, Valid ID (front and back), and a selfie with the ID. Always check the form for the exact requirements.

How will I know if my application is approved?

You’ll receive a notification inside the system. Approved fields also appear on the map and in your dashboard.

Quick Guides

  1. Register a Field → Fill details → Upload documents → Submit → Track status
  2. Join a Field (Worker) → Go to Lobby Map → Click a field pin → Click Join Field → Wait for handler approval
  3. Task Log → Open Worker → Task Logging → Fill task + photos → Save
  4. Apply Driver Badge → Open Driver Badge → Fill details → Upload docs → Submit
