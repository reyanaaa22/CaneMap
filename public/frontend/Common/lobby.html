About CaneMap

CaneMap is an agricultural decision-support platform designed to help sugarcane farmers and field workers register land, track field progress, and collaborate with the Sugar Regulatory Administration (SRA) for better transparency and productivity. Developed in collaboration with local stakeholders and SRA Ormoc Mill District, CaneMap empowers farmers through digital mapping, task coordination, and predictive analytics to make informed decisions throughout the crop cycle.

Our Mission

To empower sugarcane farmers with digital tools for better decision-making and productivity through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

To be the leading agricultural technology platform for sugarcane farming in the Philippines, driving digital transformation in agriculture.

Why is the dashboard locked?

Your dashboard is currently locked.

Choose a path below to unlock the right tools for your role.

Unlock as Worker
  1. Open the map and find a registered field near you.
  2. Click Join Field.
  3. Wait for the Handler to approve your request.
  4. Once approved, the Worker dashboard unlocks automatically.
Unlock as Handler
  1. Click Register Field in the header.
  2. Fill out field details and upload required documents.
  3. The SRA will review your submission.
  4. Once approved, your Handler tools and dashboard unlock.
Unlock as Driver
  1. Apply for a Driver Badge.
  2. Provide your transport details and required documents.
  3. Wait for SRA review and approval.
  4. Once approved, the Driver dashboard unlocks.
You're all set. Follow the steps above to unlock your tools.
